Wanderlust

Since I can remember, I love traveling and one of my hobbies is to plan trips. During my semester abroad I organized weekend trips for my group of friends almost every week. This enabled me to explore Poland and all countries around it; each weekend a new city, sometimes more than one per weekend. Until now, the biggest trip that I organized was in 2017, the Route 66 in USA, the mother road that connects Chicago to Los Angeles,it lasted 25 days and I must say that it was life changing.
